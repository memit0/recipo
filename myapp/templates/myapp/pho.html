Pho

A fusion of French and Vietnamese culinary traditions

Historical Journey

Pho emerged in Vietnam during the late 19th century, during the French colonial period. The soup is believed to be influenced by both French beef stewing techniques and Chinese rice noodle soups. It originated in the Nam Dinh province before becoming popular in Hanoi and eventually spreading throughout Vietnam and the world.

Ingredient Origins

  • Bean Sprouts - Indigenous to East and Southeast Asia
