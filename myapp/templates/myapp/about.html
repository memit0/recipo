About Recipo

Tracing the Cultural Journey of Global Cuisine

Our Mission

Recipo is dedicated to exploring the fascinating origins and evolution of beloved dishes from around the world. We believe that every recipe tells a story - a story of cultural exchange, historical events, and human innovation.

What We Do

  • Track the historical journey of dishes across different cultures and continents
  • Explore the origins of key ingredients in popular recipes
  • Document how recipes have evolved and adapted across different regions
  • Map the global influence and spread of culinary traditions

Future Plans

We're working on integrating interactive maps to visualize the journey of each dish, along with expanding our recipe database to cover more cuisines and cultural intersections.
