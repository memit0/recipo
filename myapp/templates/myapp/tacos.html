Tacos

From ancient Mexican tradition to global street food

Historical Journey

Tacos have been a staple of Mexican cuisine since pre-Hispanic times, when indigenous peoples would wrap small fish in corn tortillas. The word "taco" itself originated in the 18th century Mexican silver mines, where it referred to small charges used to excavate ore. Modern tacos evolved in Mexico's working-class communities and gained international popularity through Mexican-American cuisine.
